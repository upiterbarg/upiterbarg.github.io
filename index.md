Hey there! I'm a **rising senior** studying [course 18C](https://math.mit.edu/academics/undergrad/major/course18c.php) at MIT. 



## About Me

I am broadly interested in **representation learning**, **approximate Bayesian inference**, and the design of **optimal decision-making** schemes for environments with complex dynamics.

## Research

I am currently an undergraduate research assistant in [Josh Tenenbaum](https://web.mit.edu/cocosci/josh.html)'s lab, studying (1) the **cognitive bases of strategy learning**, (2) **hierarchical reinforcement learning**, and (3) **deep learning algorithms** for **fluid manipulation tasks**.

This coming fall, I will be joining the [Climate Modeling Alliance](https://clima.caltech.edu/) to work with [Raffaele Ferrari](http://ferrari.mit.edu/) on leveraging **machine learning** to develop novel parameterizations of **turbulence** and **stochastic dynamics** in large-scale planetary flows as an [MIT Center for Computational Science and Engineering](https://cce.mit.edu/) [Advanced Undergraduate Research Scholar](https://superurop.mit.edu/).
